Version 1.0.0 - Initial Release

Welcome to Home Inventory! This first release includes:

• Complete item cataloging with barcode scanning
• Photo documentation for all items
• Warranty tracking with expiration notifications
• Receipt scanning and storage
• Budget tracking and spending analytics
• Collections for organizing related items
• Advanced search with filters
• CSV export for insurance documentation
• Face ID/Touch ID security
• Full offline support
• iCloud sync across devices
